Research Overview

My expertise lies in the field image processing techniques for harmonizing
diffusion MRI acquisitions through empirical and deep learning methods.
Please find a few highlighted projects below.

Pandora White Matter Bundle Atlas

The Pandora Atlas is a population based white matter bundle atlas created from thousands of subjects' diffusion MRI acquisitions.
It provides bundle definitions from six state-of-the-art automated techniques: TractSeg, RecoBundles, Xtract, Tracula, AFQ, and AFQ clipped.

Empirical Fieldmapping for Gradient Nonlinearity Correction

Gradient nonlinearities in MRI systems can cause distortions during diffusion MRI acquisitions. This empirical field mapping procedure allows for correcting these errors when the manufacturer specifications are unknown.
